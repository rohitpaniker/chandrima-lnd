Startup landscape — non-fintech L&D hiring trends, Bangalore 2026

Consumer tech & e-commerce

Meesho, Apna, Jumbotail, and ElasticRun are all expanding L&D and HRBP leadership. Post-Series B scale-ups in this cluster average 300–800% headcount growth in 24 months, creating urgent demand for structured capability frameworks. Quick-commerce and B2B marketplace platforms are the hottest hiring pockets for CX Training Heads.

SaaS & HealthTech

LeadSquared (Series D, $153M) and HealthPlix are aggressively building People Operations layers. SaaS firms above 1,000 employees are now institutionalising L&D as a P&L function — not just an HR sub-team. Expect VP of People and Head of Enablement roles in these companies within Q2–Q3 2026.

Logistics & supply chain

Zypp Electric, Porter, and ElasticRun are scaling last-mile operations into Tier-2 cities and the UAE — driving demand for Process Training Heads and Operations Enablement Managers. Logistics companies show the highest hiring velocity for L&D leadership roles at 8–12 years experience.

D2C & consumer goods

D2C brands like Mamaearth and Lenskart (adjacent hubs) are actively building Customer Excellence teams from Bangalore bases. HSR Layout and Koramangala remain the primary hiring zones for People Capability roles as D2C brands centralise HR ops post Series C.
