Market insights — L&D hiring trends 2026

Fintech & Financial Services

Fintech firms like Bajaj Finserv, Piramal Finance, Yes Bank, and Axis Bank are aggressively scaling L&D infrastructure to support rapid branch and product expansion. Senior L&D managers here command cross-functional mandates spanning product training, onboarding, and CX coaching — making them direct decision-makers for external training leadership talent.

GCC / MNC Technology

Global Capability Centers (Morgan Stanley, Fidelity National Financial, SAP) are investing heavily in India-specific L&D operations as headcount grows. These roles often report to global CLOs and carry significant budget authority. GCC L&D heads frequently hire externally for niche capability-building programmes and instructional design leads.

High-growth startups

Startups (Blinkit, Urban Company, Homelane) are building L&D functions from scratch post-Series B/C. Heads of Training here are generalists who own the entire learning ecosystem — from LMS selection to frontline training delivery. They are active on LinkedIn, often post hiring signals, and respond well to outbound personalized to their scale challenges.

Key hiring themes

Across all three segments, demand is highest for: (1) instructional designers with LMS expertise, (2) CX training specialists for frontline/contact-center teams, (3) L&D operations leads who can manage vendors and content pipelines. Profiles with Fintech + CX coaching backgrounds are the most sought-after combination heading into H2 2026.
